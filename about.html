WHAT IS GRSS

GRSS stands for Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society. The fields of interest of the GRS Society are the theory, concepts, and techniques of science and engineering as they apply to the remote sensing of the earth, oceans, atmosphere, space, as well as the processing, interpretation and dissemination of this information. Remote sensing process, inverse scattering, processing of geoscientific data, image processing, artificial intelligence, and data interpretation techniques.
